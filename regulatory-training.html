EMFA masterclass

This training offers in three online sessions a comprehensive overview of the new EU rules contained in the recently adopted European Media Freedom Act (EMFA). These rules will affect media service providers (including commercial televisions, public service media, streaming companies and the press industry), device manufacturers, audience measurement organisations, large social media
companies, ministries and regulators.

Download the course programme

Topics covered include :

  • Context of adoption of the EMFA, its overall aim and structure
  • New rights for media service providers
  • Rules to safeguard transparency and editorial independence of media services
  • New rights for citizens
  • Audience measurement and state advertising
  • Insitutional changes

postal and delivery masterclass

The postal sector is changing fast. In our two-day masterclass, we provide an introduction to the critical trends in the sector – including the decline in traditional postal volumes and the growth in parcel deliveries resulting from e-commerce – and how these changes will inform the future of postal regulation. Topics covered include the future of universal service, regulating parcel deliveries and e-commerce, climate and environmental challenges, and more.

Register now
Download the course programme

Topics covered include :

  • A historical perspective on EU postal regulation
  • The evolution of universal service
  • Labour costs and regulation
  • E-commerce regulation
  • Digitalisation and digital inclusion
  • Last mile delivery
  • Climate and environmental challenges
