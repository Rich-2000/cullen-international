conferences & seminars

Hear from experts across industry and government in our seminars and conferences. Cullen International runs regular events to help our clients get an all-round perspective on the latest developments in regulation and competition law, as well as network with experts and peers. Our team members are also available to book as speakers at external conferences and events. Find out more about our events and conference appearances below.

discover all our events

BITS seminars

Our Brussels Internet and Telecoms Seminars (BITS) provide a platform for the EU’s top regulation experts to discuss new developments and emerging issues across telecoms and the digital economy. Get your intelligence from the front-line of telecoms and internet regulation.

regulatory briefings

Our regulatory briefings are designed to help clients get up to speed rapidly on relevant topics, with talks by experts, Q&A sessions, and an opportunity to network with other Cullen International subscribers.
